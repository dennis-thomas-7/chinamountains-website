Huangshan Mountain

Huangshan, literally meaning the Yellow Mountain, is a mountain range in southern Anhui Province in eastern China.

Songshan Mountain

Mount Song is a mountain in central China's Henan Province, along the southern bank of the Yellow River.

Tianzi Mountain

Tianzi Mountain is a mountain in Zhangjiajie in the Hunan Province of China, close to Suoxi Valley in South China.
